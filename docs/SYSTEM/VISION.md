# VISION.md - 3kpro.services Company Strategy

Last Updated: 2026-01-09

---

## Company Purpose

**3kpro.services** is an IT solutions company and SaaS product factory based in Tulsa, OK.

We build tools that solve real problems for SMBs and creators, then sell them directly through our marketplace.

---

## Business Model

**Three Revenue Streams:**

1. **IT Solutions & Consulting**
   - Website development
   - Cloud solutions
   - Custom development
   - Data management
   - Cybersecurity
   - Mobile solutions

2. **SaaS Product Sales**
   - XELORA (flagship): AI-powered viral content intelligence
   - Cloud Ledger: Financial analysis tool
   - n8n Workflow Marketplace: Pre-built automation bundles
   - Azure Cost Optimizer: SMB cloud cost audits
   - OAuth Token Manager: Token health dashboard for agencies
   - (More products from Dev/ department pipeline)

3. **Marketplace Platform**
   - Direct Stripe payments
   - One-time purchases + subscriptions
   - Productized services (no custom consulting)

---

## Target Customers

**Primary:**
- Small-to-medium businesses (1-50 employees)
- Content creators & agencies
- "Accidental IT admins" (small biz owners managing their own tech)

**Secondary:**
- Developers needing tools/workflows
- Automation engineers (n8n/Zapier users)

---

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Product Factory Model**
   - Agentic workflow scaffolds products systematically (Dev/ department)
   - Each product has dedicated AI agent (siloed, focused)
   - Proven with XELORA → now scalable

2. **Direct Sales, No Middlemen**
   - Stripe integration = instant monetization
   - No AppSumo/Gumroad dependency (optional channels)
   - Full customer relationship ownership

3. **Tulsa-Based, SMB-Focused**
   - Understand small business pain points
   - Affordable pricing ($29-$149 range)
   - No enterprise bloat

---

## Product Philosophy

**Anti-Scope (What We Don't Build):**
- Enterprise features (keep it simple)
- Custom consulting (productized only)
- Free tiers with upsell funnels (clear pricing upfront)
- Multi-cloud/multi-platform unless critical
- Features that require ongoing maintenance teams

**What We Build:**
- Tools that solve one problem exceptionally well
- Self-service products (minimal support needed)
- One-time purchases or simple subscriptions
- Clear ROI (save time/money within first use)
